Data Dive: Soda consumption trends in 2020–2025

Zero‑sugar is rising — but sugary beverages still have a large footprint.

By Data Reporter Science October 29, 2025

Key signals

  • Zero‑sugar category growth: Market analyses show strong growth through 2024–2025; Coca‑Cola reports Zero Sugar volume up 9% for FY2024.
  • SSB intake remains common: CDC surveillance notes substantial weekly consumption among young children (2021–2023 data), and many adults report daily SSB use.
  • Policy levers: Experimental warning labels can reduce sugary‑drink purchase intent; real‑world policy studies report modest reductions after warning/FOPlabels.

Context
